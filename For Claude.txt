### **Goal**

Develop a web-based accounting and automation system for managing apartment sales, contracts, and installment payments ‚Äî with automated tracking, analytics, and reporting.

---

## **1. Project Scope**

### **Core Objectives**

* Centralize apartment, customer, and contract data.
* Automate calculation of payments, balances, and remaining installments.
* Generate reports and dashboards for administrators and accountants.
* Provide user-friendly portals for customers, accountants, and agents.
* Exclude payment gateway integration (manual payment tracking only).

---

## **2. Main Features**

### **A. Apartment & Contract Management**

* CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for apartments.
* Contract creation with linked customer and apartment.
* Auto-calculate total price, duration, and monthly payment.
* Status tracking (active, completed, overdue).

---

### **B. Customer & Agent Management**

* Store customer information (name, contact, passport, address).
* Store agent details and commissions.
* Link each customer to a contract and agent.
* Agent performance dashboard (number of sales, commission earned).

---

### **C. Installment & Payment Tracking**

* Automatic calculation of monthly installments.
* Record manual payments (cash or bank).
* Show paid vs unpaid months, and remaining balance.
* Auto-detect overdue payments and highlight in dashboard.
* Display complete payment history for each contract.

---

### **D. Dashboards & Analytics**

* Overview of:

  * Total apartments sold / available.
  * Total amount received vs. pending.
  * Monthly revenue trends.
  * Overdue payments and top-performing agents.
* Interactive charts (e.g., bar, line, pie charts).

---

### **E. Reports & Data Export**

* Filterable reports by:

  * Date range
  * Agent
  * Building/block
  * Payment status
* Export reports to Excel and PDF.
* Generate tax or accounting summary reports.

---

### **F. Notifications & Alerts (Internal only)**

* System reminders for overdue payments.
* Dashboard indicators (e.g., red alerts for overdue customers).
* Optional daily summary emails to admin (optional feature).

---

### **G. Role-Based Access Control**

| Role           | Permissions                           |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Admin**      | Full access to all data and users     |
| **Accountant** | Manage contracts, payments, reports   |
| **Agent**      | Manage personal clients and contracts |
| **Customer**   | View own payment status and contract  |

---

### **H. Customer Portal**

* Customer login with secure access.
* View contract details, payment schedule, and remaining balance.
* View or download receipts and contract copies.
* No online payment ‚Äî only view and history.

---

## **3. Technical Architecture**
follow Solid!
### **Frontend**

* **Framework:** React (with TypeScript)
* **UI:** Tailwind CSS or Bootstrap
* **Charts:** Recharts or Chart.js
* **State management:** Redux Toolkit or Context API

### **Backend**

* **Framework:** ASP.NET Core Web API (.NET 8)
* **Architecture:** Clean Architecture (Controllers ‚Üí Services ‚Üí Repository ‚Üí EF Core)
* **Authentication:** ASP.NET Identity + JWT Tokens
* **Mapping:** AutoMapper
* **Logging:** Serilog
* **Database ORM:** Entity Framework Core

### **Database**

* **System:** Microsoft SQL Server
* **Entities:**

  * Apartments
  * Customers
  * Contracts
  * Payments
  * Agents
  * InstallmentPlans
